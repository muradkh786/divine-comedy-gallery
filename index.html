The Divine Comedy

The Divine Comedy is an epic poem written by the famous Dante Aligheri. Who was an Italian and resident of Florence. Living a his life as politician he was banished by those who won favor in their court. He began to travel never able to return to his beloved Florence. On this journey he took a journey that would allow him to reveal his misgivings and show his personal journey to finding redemption for his mistakes in life.
