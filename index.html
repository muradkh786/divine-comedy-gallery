The Divine Comedy

The Divine Comedy is an epic poem written by the famous Dante Aligheri. Who was an Italian and resident of Florence. Living his life as a politician he was banished by those who won favor in their court. He began to travel never able to return to his beloved Florence. He took a journey that would allow him to reveal his misgivings and show his personal journey to finding redemption for his mistakes in life.
